About iFire Protection

We're a Southern California team focused on wildfire prevention and resilience. Our approach blends practical, field-tested tactics with data-driven design to deliver right-sized protection for homes, schools, and businesses in the Wildland-Urban Interface.

Founded in response to California's escalating wildfire crisis, iFire Protection brings together expertise in fire science, hydraulic engineering, and emergency response planning. We believe that proactive hardening and intelligent system design can dramatically improve outcomes when wildfires threaten communities.

Leadership

Tyler Grasmick & Chris Figureida lead our team with combined decades of experience in wildland firefighting, system design, and private emergency response.

Core Competencies:

  • Wildland mitigation & HIZ assessments
  • Exterior sprinkler & foam systems
  • Private resource planning & readiness
  • NFPA-informed engineering
  • Red-flag event protocols

Our Philosophy

Every property has unique vulnerabilities and resources. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. Instead, we assess your specific exposure, water supply, structures, and budget to design systems that deliver maximum protection for your investment.

We prioritize the improvements that matter most. Rather than overwhelming clients with lengthy checklists, we identify the highest-impact mitigations first and help you build a phased approach that fits your timeline and resources.

What sets us apart

Clear engineering, transparent pricing, and a focus on the highest-impact improvements first.

Our Differentiators:
  • Field Experience: We've worked actual wildfire incidents and understand real-world conditions
  • Engineering Rigor: All designs account for hydraulics, flow rates, and system performance
  • Cost Transparency: Detailed proposals with no hidden fees
  • Local Knowledge: Deep familiarity with SoCal fire behavior and regional regulations
