Sprinkler & Foam Systems

Custom roofline and perimeter sprinkler systems with optional Class A+ foam proportioning. We size pumps, hose lays, and nozzles to your available water supply and target coverage areas. Designs consider friction loss, elevation head, and run-time to ensure reliable performance.

What We Deliver:

  • Design drawings & bill of materials
  • Pump & flow testing report
  • Seasonal service & maintenance
  • NFPA-informed best practices
  • Foam concentrate recommendations

System Components:

Roofline Coverage

Overhead sprinklers positioned to wet roof surfaces and create thermal barrier during ember storms.

Perimeter Defense

Ground-level sprinklers protecting structures, decks, and vegetation buffer zones.

Foam Injection

Class A foam proportioning systems for enhanced penetration and longer protection duration.

Home Ignition Zone (HIZ) Assessments

Comprehensive audits of near-home fuels, ember entry points, and material vulnerabilities. You receive a prioritized mitigation plan with estimated impact vs. cost for each recommendation.

Three-Zone Evaluation:

Immediate Zone (0-5 ft)
  • Vent screening
  • Roof materials assessment
  • Deck & siding evaluation
  • Window vulnerability
  • Ember entry points
Intermediate Zone (5-30 ft)
  • Vegetation management
  • Fuel spacing standards
  • Tree pruning guidelines
  • Mulch & ground cover
  • Outbuilding hardening
Extended Zone (30-100 ft)
  • Fuel break placement
  • Ladder fuel removal
  • Defensible space design
  • Fire-resistant landscaping
  • Access & egress planning

Assessment Deliverables:

  • Detailed site evaluation report with photos
  • Prioritized action items ranked by impact
  • Cost estimates for recommended improvements
  • Compliance review for local fire codes
  • Follow-up consultation included

Planning Services:

  • Hose lay planning & cache placement
